An Interview with Kelly Ann Deline

First question; you're planning on designing your own website, correct? What is it for?

It's for a travel blog.

Have you come up with any ideas on how you want it laid out and are there any specific websites that have inspired your ideas for it?

Yes. There's a website called 'My Perfect Itinerary', and one called 'The Bucket List Family' and both of those, um, kind of inspired my preferred layout.

Okay, what do you think is your favourite part of the website, or will be?

Probably the destinations page.

Why is that?

Because it will be a log where I can go back and look and see all the different places that I've been, and places that I've stayed; with pictures and also information.

Cool! Have you ever done a project like this before?

Sort of? In college I had to do an online portfolio, [where] I used Wix, which is just like a... website building service I guess? But it was like just for a mark, it wasn't... similar to the travel blog idea.

What inspired you or drove you to begin something like this?

I travel quite a bit and I figured my knowledge was, um... my experience and knowledge was something that I should share with the world. That's pretty much it.

Do you plan on expanding outside of the website in any way? As in like books or Youtube or anything?

I would love to expand into Youtube. Obviously instagram, facebook, twitter, um, different social media. And then if it ever took off really big, maybe into merchandise?

Ending off, what advice can you offer to someone who wants to do the same thing and isn't really sure where to start?

I would say start by looking at websites that are similar to the one you want to make, like similar topics, and just kind of get inspiration from there.

Thanks!
